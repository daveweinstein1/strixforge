Benchmarks vs. Reality

Data: January 2026 | Source: Verified Reviews | Author: Dave Weinstein (@daveweinstein1)

StrixForge is a software project; we do not maintain a hardware validation lab. The data below is aggregated from the "Big Tech" review cycle of late 2025 (RTX 50-series launch) and the Strix Halo launch reviews from January 2026. This report focuses on the metrics that matter: Tokens/sec and Cost Efficiency.

The PCIe 5.0 Improvement: The RTX 5090 is significantly faster at offloading than the 4090 was (6.2 t/s vs 3.1 t/s) thanks to the saturated PCIe 5.0 x16 bus. However, it is still 2.4x slower than Strix Halo because it must constantly shuttle 10GB of weights back and forth to system RAM.

Conclusion

The 2026 data is clear:

  • NVIDIA owns the training market and "small model" inference (8B/34B models fly on the 5090).
  • Apple owns the creative professional and high-budget research market.
  • Strix Halo owns the "Independent Lab." It is the only hardware that makes 70B/100B models accessible without spending $5,000.
